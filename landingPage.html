About Me

I'm a Human-Centered Design and Development student, hoping to becomg a professional UX Designer. I design interfaces so that end users have a better experience with digital products. I first entered the industry after a couple of switches in my area of study. I first wanted to strictly Information Technology, but after finding out about User Experience Design, which focuses on empathizing with users in order to make the functions of websites easier and more efficient, I know I wanted to switch. I'm happy to be able to combine a desire to understand others with my background in technology.
